The Artist

With over a decade of experience in the industry, I have dedicated my life to the craft of tattooing. My work focuses on the intersection of dark art and organic flow.

I believe that every tattoo should be a unique expression of the individual wearing it. Whether you're looking for a small, meaningful piece or a full bodysuit, I approach every project with the same level of dedication and detail.
