Services & Pricing

Transparent guidelines for your investment.

Custom Design

Hourly Rate: $150/hr

Completely unique pieces designed from scratch based on your consultation. Perfect for sleeves, back pieces, and complex concepts. Includes one revision of the artwork.

Minimum booking: 2 hours.

Flash Tattoos

Flat Rate: $100 - $400

Pre-designed artwork available to be tattooed once. These designs are non-customizable but are priced at a flat rate. Check the 'Flash' highlights on Instagram for available designs.

Cover-Ups & Blast Overs

Hourly Rate: $180/hr

Specialized service to hide or rework old tattoos. Requires an in-person consultation to assess feasibility. These sessions often require more time and skill.

Consultations

Free (30 Mins)

A face-to-face or video chat to discuss your ideas, placement, and pricing. Required for all large-scale custom work.

Studio Policies

  • Deposits: A non-refundable deposit of $100 is required to secure all appointments. This comes off the final price.
  • Cancellations: Must be made at least 48 hours in advance to reschedule without losing your deposit.
  • Guests: Please come alone due to studio space limitations.
  • Sobriety: We will not tattoo anyone under the influence of drugs or alcohol.
