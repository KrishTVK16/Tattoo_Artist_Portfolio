Common Questions

Curious about the process? Here's what you need to know.

How do I book an appointment?

+

Booking is done via our Booking Page. A non-refundable deposit is required to secure your date. Please describe your idea in detail and attach references if possible.

What are your rates?

+

I charge an hourly rate for large scale work and a flat piece price for smaller, one-shot tattoos. Please visit the Pricing Page for a detailed breakdown.

Does it hurt?

+

Yes, but it is manageable. Pain tolerance varies by person and placement. We recommend a good night's sleep and a hearty meal before your session to keep your blood sugar stable.

How should I prepare for my session?

+

Avoid alcohol for 24 hours prior. Hydrate well. Wear comfortable clothing that allows easy access to the area being tattooed. Bring snacks and water for longer sessions.

What is your cancellation policy?

+

We require at least 48 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Deposits are forfeited for last-minute cancellations or no-shows.
