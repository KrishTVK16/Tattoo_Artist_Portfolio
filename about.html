The Artist

Elena Voss

I am a resident artist at InkRealm Studio, specializing in fine line realism and macabre aesthetics. With over 8 years of experience, my work explores the duality of life and decay.

My journey began in traditional illustration before moving to skin as my primary medium. I believe that a tattoo is an energy exchange—a ritual of permanence in a temporary world.

Safety & Hygiene

Your safety is paramount. The studio adheres to strict hospital-grade sterilization protocols.

Single Use

All needles, cartridges, and ink caps are single-use disposables. Opened in front of you.

Sterilization

All non-disposable equipment is autoclave sterilized. Surfaces are wrapped and disinfected.

Certified

Fully licensed by the Health Department. Bloodborne Pathogens certified annually.
