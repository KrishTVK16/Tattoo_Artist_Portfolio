The Ultimate Aftercare Guide

By InkRealm  |  Oct 12, 2023

Getting a new tattoo is exciting, but the work doesn't stop once you leave the chair. The healing process is critical to ensuring your ink looks crisp and vibrant for years to come. Here is our comprehensive guide to taking care of your fresh piece.

1. The First 24 Hours

Leave the bandage on for at least 3-4 hours. When you remove it, wash the area gently with lukewarm water and unscented anti-bacterial soap. Do not scrub. Pat dry with a clean paper towel.

2. Hydration is Key

Apply a thin layer of specialized tattoo ointment or unscented lotion (like Aquaphor or Lubriderm) 2-3 times a day. Do not over-saturate; the skin needs to breathe.

3. What to Avoid

For the first two weeks, avoid:

  • Direct sunlight
  • Swimming (pools, ocean, hot tubs)
  • Tight clothing that rubs against the tattoo
  • Picking or scratching scabs

4. Long Term Care

Once healed, sunscreen is your best friend. UV rays break down ink pigment over time. Protect your art to keep it looking fresh.
