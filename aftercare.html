Client Care System

Your piece is an investment. Protect it.

Healing Timeline

DAY 1-3: Fresh & Weeping

The Bandage Phase

Keep the bandage/Saniderm on for the time specified by your artist (usually 3-4 hours or 3-4 days for Saniderm). When removing, do so under warm water. Wash gently with antibacterial soap. Pat dry with paper towel. Do not rub.

DAY 4-14: The Itch

Peeling & Flaking

The tattoo will start to peel like a sunburn. This is normal. DO NOT PICK OR SCRATCH. Apply a very thin layer of recommended moisturizer 2-3 times a day.

DAY 15-30: Settling

The Silver Skin

The top layer has healed, but lower layers are recovering. The skin may look slightly cloudy or shiny. Keep moisturizing and protecting from the sun.

DO

  • Wash hands before touching the tattoo.
  • Wear loose, breathable clothing.
  • Keep it out of the sun.
  • Stay hydrated.

DON'T

  • Submerge in water (No baths, pools, ocean).
  • Pick at scabs or flakes.
  • Listen to your friends' advice over your artist's.
  • Apply heavy petroleum jelly (unless directed).
